### sagit changes ###
New eucalyptus Kernel:
Features:
*Undervolt, Overclock, GPU step and GPU at higher bus
*Frandom support
*USB-FastCharge
*Adreno idler
*Adreno boost
*Added CPU Governors alucardsched, darknesssched, energy_dcfc, helix_schedutil, pwrutilx and schedalucard
*Added I/O scheduler noop, zen, fiops, sio, sioplus, tripndroid and maple
*Dynamic schedtune
*Fsync support
*Control for sched features
